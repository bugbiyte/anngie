About me

Tech-producer by day, cat lady by night. Music junkie. A coffee drinker. A mover. A shaker. Sometimes at the same time. Not a nerd, but invest in many things nerds also like. I'm also a writer whose editorial has been featured in a variety of online publications, including MP3.com, CBS's TV.com, Radio.com, and Last.fm. In my spare time, I'm working on conquering web development. I also like to paint, shoot video, and snap pretty pictures.
